Bio

I am a PostDoctoral researcher at the Programming Group at the University of St. Gallen (HSG) in St. Gallen, Switzerland, and a scientific collaborator at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Lugano, Switzerland. Before that, I spent 5 years as a PostDoctoral researcher at the Software Institute at the Università della Svizzera italiana (USI) in Lugano, Switzerland working on the Precrime ERC Advanced Grant project led by professor Paolo Tonella. I obtained by Ph.D. from Università degli studi di Genova, Genova, Italy, in a joint collaboration with Fondazione Bruno Kessler in Trento, Italy, under the supervision of Paolo Tonella and Filippo Ricca.

Research Interests

I am interested in software testing with a particular focus on test generation. I focus on testing AI systems/models to make them more robust and on using AI to improve software testing.
